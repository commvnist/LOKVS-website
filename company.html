Company

Ottawa-based early-stage autonomy infrastructure development.

LOKVS builds practical sensing and estimation components for teams that need reliable relative spatial intelligence in deployed systems. We focus on subsystem readiness, not consumer-facing positioning products.

What we build

Modular sensing modules, embedded firmware for estimation and signal integrity, and explicit developer-facing interface contracts for integration and validation.

What we are not

A gadget company or a generic defense marketing brand. We are an engineering supplier for mission-relevant systems teams.

Why now

The market is moving toward mobile, cooperative autonomy where fixed localization assumptions and ad hoc interfaces are no longer enough.

Principles

  • Interface contracts before feature surface area.
  • Topology-change and role-change support at every stage.
  • Design for integration confidence: timing, diagnostics, and reproducible behavior.
  • Platform roadmap beyond UWB into additional sensing and perception modules.
